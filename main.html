Welcome to Dagher Engineering.
Dagher Engineering is an award-winning building systems engineering and sustainability consulting firm based in New York City. Our portfolio includes work in the commercial office + retail, cultural, educational, healthcare, hospitality, and residential market sectors for both U.S-based and international clients. Though our team members have the technical expertise to design systems for virtually any building type, we specialize in projects requiring a high degree of coordination due to their complexity, scope, or sustainability goals.

    Philosophy:
    We understand the issues surrounding the built environment today and how building systems can contribute to a sustainable future. We also recognize that as a New York firm, we can effect change that impacts future generations. This understanding gives us the passion and purpose to strive for true excellence in engineering design.     Our
    Approach:
    To achieve excellence in engineering, we offer personalized service to every client, working with the entire project team to develop a unique solution for each project. We get involved in the design process early and begin by actively listening to our client's needs. From there, we have developed an iterative design process that requires working closely with the project team to achieve a well-coordinated, integrated design.

    Our project managers are both technical experts and exceptional communicators, allowing them to work effectively with owners, developers, architects, and other consultants. Our working principals oversee this process to ensure that the project team's original goals are met. It is through this collaborative and holistic project approach that we achieve excellence in engineering design.
